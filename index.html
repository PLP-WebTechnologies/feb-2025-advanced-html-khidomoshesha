A Cabbage Farm in Free State

You have come to the right place! Hey there, welcome to all things Cabbage Farming.

Welcome to our cabbage farm, located in the heart of the Free State Province. We take pride in producing high-quality, fresh cabbages that are grown with care and dedication.

In this season we offer high qualilty Taurus variety cabbages, which are known for their excellent taste, crisp texture, and long shelf life. Whether you're a wholesaler, retailer, or an individual looking for fresh produce, we've got you covered.

Each cabbage is available for just R15, ensuring you get the best value for your money. Come and experience the freshness straight from our farm!

We also offer the following services:

  1. Seedlings for farmers looking to grow their own crops
  2. Bulk sales for wholesalers and retailers
  3. Delivery services (if available)
  4. Farming consultation on best practices for cabbage farming
  5. Partnership opportunities with local markets and stores
  6. Organic or pesticide-free options for health-conscious customers
